# civichack

Our goal as the City Megaminds is to make accessibility accessible. Using the Cyvl Database we are displaying pavement and sidewalk quality to let people be informed about the city of Boston. We are starting this representation in the scenic neighborhood of Brookline, MA.

During the 2026 Civic Hackathon our team develed into the City-Tech track to have news and infastructure quality all in one easy to reach place.
